Our Origin

Born from a different kind of rock bottom.

Ankur Warikoo started Money Matters because of financial rock bottom. Calm+Lab started because of something harder to name — six months of living inside a body that felt like it was shutting down.

Hair falling out. Constant headaches. Seven kilos gone. Head down, hands in pockets, not wanting to talk to anyone. Stopping at mirrors because the person looking back wasn't recognisable anymore.

How Calm+Lab works

We profile you on 6 parameters.
Then we grow — one point at a time.

Stress isn't one thing. It shows up differently in your sleep, your food, your body, your screen time, your emotions, and your thoughts. So we track all six — not to overwhelm you, but to understand where it's actually coming from.

We improve slowly on each point. The goal isn't perfection — it's getting to a place where stress doesn't overwhelm you anymore.

Sleep Score /10
🌙

Sleep

Quality, duration, and consistency of rest. Poor sleep and stress feed each other — we break that cycle first.

Nutrition Score /10
🥗

Nutrition

What you eat when you're stressed says a lot. We look at patterns, not perfection — and find small changes that stick.

Physical Activity Score /10
🏃

Physical Activity

Movement is one of the most underrated stress tools. Even small amounts matter — we build from wherever you are.

Digital Behaviour Score /10
📱

Digital Behaviour

Screen time, doomscrolling, comparison traps. Your phone might be adding more stress than you realise.

Emotional Wellbeing Score /10
💚

Emotional Wellbeing

How you're actually feeling — not how you say you are. We create space to name it, understand it, and work through it.

Thought Pattern Score /10
🧠

Thought Patterns

The stories we tell ourselves. Catastrophising, rumination, self-blame — we identify the patterns that keep stress alive.

Your total score? It tells you where you stand today — and more importantly, where you can grow.
